The Journey

The story begins with the owner when he came and lived for several months in a remote place, he saw that there were many farmers and organizations that had quality, but they were hindered by technology so they only sold their products to local buyers, suddenly the owner thought and found an idea for brought their products to the international market, namely exports and the Zsers Trade company was born until now which has gone through several long stories starting from educating farmers and building connections with people who will participate in the progress of Zsers Trade in the future. And with this noble goal, we have the principle of making a fair and comfortable transaction for all parties
